About Me

I'm Abhishek Bajpai, currently based in Cardiff, Wales and I'm originally from India. I've been a photographer since 2014, passionate about capturing the pure essence of human expressions and the natural connections between families and couples. I love creating cinematic frames that tell a story, blending creativity with a sharp eye for detail. With skills in Photoshop, Lightroom, and Premiere Pro, I bring photos and videos to life through thoughtful editing and content creation. Whether it's a quiet moment or a bold emotion, I'm all about turning it into something beautiful and lasting.

My vision involves sourcing diverse locations and unique props to achieve the perfect portrait, capturing the essence of each concept. This creative process excites me and allows me to express my artistic vision fully.

What inspires me most is the raw, unfiltered emotion shared between people and the fleeting moments that pass in the blink of an eye — moments that will never return. My photography is about capturing those genuine connections between loved ones, preserving the warmth, laughter, and tenderness that make life meaningful. Through a creative and cinematic lens, I aim to tell stories that feel real, timeless, and deeply human.

Photography and filmmaking have always been my passions. Unfortunately, various family commitments prevented me from pursuing them professionally until now. However, I have never lost sight of my dream. The process of creating visual stories is something that never tires me and continually unleashes my creativity. Turning this passion into a profession is not just a career choice for me; it’s a calling.
